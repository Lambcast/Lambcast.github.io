Welcome

I'm Alan Lamb, an applied economist and MPA focused on forecasting, environmental economics, and evidence-based policy design. This site is where I publish forecasts, explain my reasoning, and explore the policy implications of uncertainty.

What I Do

My work combines forecasting discipline with applied economic analysis. I build transparent, testable forecasts and use them to inform policy, long-term planning, and strategic decision-making. I’m especially interested in environmental and regional economic dynamics.

Latest Work

I’m currently developing a series of forecasts and accompanying essays that examine economic and environmental outcomes in the Mountain West. These will appear here as they are completed.
