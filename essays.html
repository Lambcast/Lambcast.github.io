Overview

This section collects essays, reflections, and longer-form reasoning tied to my forecasting work. My goal is to connect disciplined forecasting with practical insight—especially for issues that matter to the Mountain West and the communities I know best.

Topics I Explore

My essays cover a wide range of subjects, but they share a common thread: using forecasting and applied economics to understand uncertainty and guide better decisions. Areas of focus include:

  • Forecasting questions from public platforms and research communities
  • AI integration, governance, and long-term technological impacts
  • Conservation policy and environmental economics
  • Regional economic development in the Northwest and Montana
  • Decision-making under uncertainty and structured reasoning
